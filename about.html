About

Ola is a curator of journeys, especially the journeys of those on the 'pursuit of happiness'. Infusing his love of design with brand consulting and management, Ola advices, develops strategic market plans and campaigns, which it's target audiences can connect to.

Specializing in social media & webpage management,graphic design (logos, flyers etc) and photography. He collaborates with small businesses, enterprenuers and organisations to expand their reach and find their brand voices, whilst taking note and records of their brand journeys. He also uses these skills to build and update his very own platform: XOFirm.
